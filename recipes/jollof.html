Description

The food above is a ghanaian main delicacy enjoyed by millions of people and its likely to be served to you if you foind yourself in the country.

Ingrdient

  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Fresh Pepper
  • Fried Chicken
  • Fresh Onion
  • Long Grain Rice
  • Fresh Garlic bulb
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Spicies and Salt

Steps

  1. Cut your vegetable; Onions, Pepper, Tomatos etc
  2. Blind the Tomatoes, Pepper, and Onions
  3. Pour a reasonable amount of the vegetable oil into your cooking ware
  4. Pour your blended ingredient into the oil and fry
  5. Prepare your gravy to taste
  6. Now boil your rice with the cooked gravy
  7. Serve with the fried chicken and some vegetable
